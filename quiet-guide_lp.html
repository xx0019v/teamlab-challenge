01 — Manifesto

The interface should not become the experience. It should stay behind it. Guiding only when needed. Silent when it is not.

アプリは主役にならず、作品体験の裏側で静かに来場者を支える。必要なときだけ現れ、それ以外のときは沈黙する。

02

The problem with light in the dark.

In an immersive exhibition, the experience is the darkness, the sound, the movement of the body, and the distance to the artwork.

A bright, information-dense app interface breaks that immersion the moment it is opened. The screen becomes the brightest object in the room.

没入型展示では、暗闇・音・身体の動き・作品との距離が体験の中心になる。明るく情報量の多い通常のUIは、開いた瞬間にその没入を壊してしまう。だからこそ、暗所でも迷わず、作品を邪魔しないガイドUIを設計した。

03

Three quiet principles.

01

Low Brightness

Deep black surfaces, a single water-blue accent used only where it carries meaning. Nothing decorative emits light.

背景は深い黒、色は意味のある場所だけ。装飾は光らない。

02

Less Text

Information appears only when needed. In front of an artwork, the screen holds direction, distance, and one short hint.

情報は必要なときだけ。作品の前では方向・距離・短いヒントだけ。

03

Move with the Visitor

Large tap areas, current location and the next artwork first. A guide you can follow without staring at the screen.

現在地と次の作品を優先。画面を見続けなくても歩けるUI。
