QUIET GUIDE — Cover
01 / Manifesto

The interface should not become the experience.
It should stay behind it.
Guiding only when needed. Silent when it is not.

01
Low Brightness
Restrained contrast. One accent. No decoration.
低輝度・低彩度・装飾なし。
02
Less Text During Immersion
Only direction, distance, and one short hint remain.
方向・距離・短いヒントだけが残る。
03
Movement-first Guidance
Large tap areas. Short directions. Vibration over sound.
大きなタップ領域・短い指示・音より振動。

A simplified map that prioritizes location, direction, and the nearest works. In dark spaces, orientation matters more than detail.
